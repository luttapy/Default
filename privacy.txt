Privacy Policy for PlotHole
Last Updated: May 31, 2026

Your privacy is paramount to us. This Privacy Policy explains how PlotHole (referred to as "the Application," "we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our software.

1. No Data Collection or Storage
PlotHole is a local library auditing tool designed to scan your personal Plex media server and identify missing episodes.

Zero Personal Data: We do not collect, request, or store any personal information, usernames, email addresses, or account credentials.

Zero Media Data: All scanning, metadata cross-referencing, and processing happen locally on your own hardware. Your library contents, file structures, and Plex API tokens never leave your local environment and are never transmitted to us or any third-party servers.

2. No Ads and No Tracking
The Application is entirely ad-free. We do not use any advertising networks, behavioral tracking, analytics cookies, or telemetry software. Your usage habits remain completely private to you.

3. Device Permissions
PlotHole operates with minimal permissions:

Network Access: Required solely to communicate locally with your Plex Media Server and to optionally fetch official episode lists from public metadata databases (like TheTVDB or TheMovieDB) to verify missing content.

No Sensitive Permissions: The Application does not require, request, or access your device’s location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage outside of the designated application directory.

4. Children’s Privacy (Users Under 13)
Because PlotHole does not collect, store, or share any personal information from any user, it is fully compliant with children’s privacy regulations, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). The Application is safe for use by individuals of all ages, including children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Since the app does not collect user data, changes will typically only reflect new local features or platform requirements.

6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to open an issue on our official project repository.
